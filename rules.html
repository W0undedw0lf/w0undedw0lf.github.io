The Rules

Each Character Has:

6 different racial abilities 4 essence slots. Each essence has 5 abilities. Each essence is tied to a stat.

The Stats

Speed

Iron Rank-40 ft of movement. 2 actions in a turn. Max of +1 extra action. Each extra action takes 2 stamina. +1 advantage to rolls involving speed. 4d6 attack die. Rank decides hit parameters. EXAMPLE-It would be 3+ on the d6 to land a success on an iron monster as an iron adventurer. It would be 4+ to land on a low bronze monster. A high bronze would take 5+

Spirit

Iron Rank-All non-physical attacks gain +1 damage. Each rank of iron will give +1 mana. May spend 2 mana to increase nonphysical damage by 1. +1 advantage to rolls involving spirit.

Recovery

Iron Rank- All Health, Mana, Stamina, regenerate at 5 per hour. (At iron rank, cant just go into trouble back to back. Have to be somewhat careful. Base regen can be buffed through essence abilities.)

Power

Iron Rank-All physical attacks gain +1 damage. May spend 1 stamina to increase physical damage by 1. +1 advantage to rolls involving power. The 4 main stats will level up through the ranks as the abilities/essence tied to them level up. Abilities level up through use. Leveling is twice as fast whilst life is on the line (fighting)

Everything Has Ranks

Everything starts at iron 0 and goes to iron 9. Abilities, stats Without essence, no bonuses. Pseudo Iron will be +1s. Iron rank is +2s

Health

(Power+Recovery)10 Iron=20 health. Bronze 60 health

Mana

[(Spirit+Recovery)10]2 Iron 40 (this equation includes Dracodalys racial)

Stamina

(Speed+Power)10

Armor and magic resist will be flat damage reduc. Certain weapons/abilities will bypass reductions.

Hitting an iron monster will be 1-2 success based on how fast they are. Hitting a bronze monster will be 3+. advantages/abilities will add more dice to the attacking rule. Disadvantages will do the opposite.

EXTRA ACTIONS

Attacking/moving same rules apply.

Reactions- Can be made on turn of threats.

Parry/dodge is speed (4d6) 4+ for same rank. Two success negate damage. 1 success halves damage

Counter, half the damage of the attack and attack back. (4d6) 4+ on same rank is success. Requires same amount of success as the attack normally would.

SKILLS

Can be gained through actions or skill books

Naturally gained skills will have +1d6 to any rolls

Skills at beginner start with 3D6, 2+-5+ will be based on difficulty of the check or what they are doing. If it is in line with skill level, like beginner skill vs beginner check it will be 3+

Skills will go as- Beginner, Proficient, Something, Expert, Something, Sage

Aura

Before iron there is no aura

At Iron an ability linked to spirit will give access to aura.

Experience

Based on situations DM can award extra exp

Non-combat Iron rank

5% for no cd, 10 small cd, 20% for large cd (Except lick wounds, consider it in combat for exp)

Combat Iron rank

Iron 0-2 10% per use, small cd 20%, large cd 50%

3-5 7%, 14%, 40%

If fighting anything bronze rank, double xp gain. If silver, triple.
